MapleStory Kaling Timer inspired by @Saltette's Kalos timer! This timer is mainly used for the Kaling Destiny Liberation Mission, but can be used to get a gist of general timings as well. Preferably, you will have someone on the side who manages the timer and shotcalls while you run the boss.


Simply click on a peril in P1 to start and timers will begin running. FMA timers in P1 dog and P2 kaling will automatically restart by themselves, but if they are delayed by bind, you can simply re-click the button to restart it at 60s. Clutch timers also automatically restart by themeselves, but if they are delayed, you can also restart them.
